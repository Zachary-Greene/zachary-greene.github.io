Mini Remote Control R2D2

This is my scaled model of the popular Star Wars droid, R2D2. I have spent a couple years 3D modelling the object. I am currently 3D printing all the pieces for R2, and have completed the designs for the PCBs (Printed Circuit Board) and they are ready to be manufactured and soldered. The components needed for the main PCB are as follows: ATmega328P Digikey x1, LD1117V33 (COM-00526) Sparkfun/Core Electronics x1, NRF24L01 x1, NPN Transistor (COM-00521) Sparkfun/Core Electronics x12, 4xAA Battery Holder (PRT-12083) Sparkfun/Core Electronics x1.

The following are optional components for a speaker: 0.1uF Ceramic Capacitor (COM-08375) Sparkfun/Core Electronics x1, 1.25K Ohm Resistor Digikey x1, 8 Ohm Speaker x1, DFPlayer Mini (DFR0299) Core Electronics x1

The following list is for the remote PCB: ATmega328P Digikey x1, LD1117V33 (COM-00526) Sparkfun/Core Electronics x1, NRF24L01 x1, 4xAA Battery Holder (PRT-12083) Sparkfun/Core Electronics x1
